About us

We're not a bakery. We're a cupcake brand.

Loui's Cupcakes by Alston Ponde-Silcott is a modern distribution concept built around one idea: great cupcakes should be available whenever and wherever you want them.

Step 01

Partner bakeries produce

We work with certified partner bakeries who produce cupcakes to our exact recipe and quality specification.

Step 02

Loui's brands & packages

Every cupcake is packaged in Loui's Cupcakes branded boxes with allergen information, ingredients, and best-before dates.

Step 03

You find us in the machine

Our vending machines are stocked fresh and placed at convenient locations — schools, shopping centres, gyms, and more.
